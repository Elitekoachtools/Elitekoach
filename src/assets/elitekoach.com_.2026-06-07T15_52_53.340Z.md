At EliteKoach, we connect fans and learners with world-class celebrities, stars and professionals for personalised coaching. Users upload videos showcasing their skills or challenges, and in return, receive expert feedback, actionable tips, and advice from icons who’ve mastered their craft.

Our advanced AI makes the experience seamless—helping users find the perfect coach for their goals and enabling coaches to create tailored content for their audience.

For users, it’s a chance to learn from the best. For coaches, it’s an opportunity to inspire and grow their influence. At EliteKoach, we’re turning ambition into achievement.

How it works

Your Journey to Mastery in 3 Steps

Upload Your Video

Capture a short video showcasing your skills, whether it’s singing, pitching, gaming, football or any activity you want to master.

Send It to Your Coach

Pick your coach from our experts, submit your request, and receive personalised, actionable advice tailored to your goals.

Get Expert Insights & Improve

Receive a video response packed with tips and strategies from your coach, helping you refine your skills and grow faster.
